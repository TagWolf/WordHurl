WordHurl

WordHurl is a word game that is a little bit Hangman, Scrabble, and a dash of Wordle sprinkled on top. Your goal is to figure out what today's word is that is hidden in the Word Tiles.

There are three main rows of tiles. The Word Tiles which is the word you are trying to figure out one guess at a time, the Guess Tiles which contains all the letters of the word hidden above (but in a random order), and History Tiles which keep a record of your hits and misses during the entire game.

Start by guessing a letter position in the Word Tile row by selecting one of the Word Tiles and then clicking the Guess Tile. The game will let you know if you got a hit or a miss and keep a record of guesses for each Word Tile. Selecting a different Word Tile will have a completely different set of hit and misses.

When you correctly guess the position of one of your scrambled Guess Tiles, the Word Tile where the Guess Tile goes will reveal itself and the Guess Tile will becoming inactive.

Continue the process of picking a Word Tile and guessing with a Guess Tile until you have uncovered the entire word.

Try to impress your friends by using the least number of guesses possible to solve the puzzle for today and share your score on social media!

Tips #1: Games reset to a new word at midnight.

Tips #2: A correct guess (hit) will case the selection box to automatically move to the next unsolved tile on the right.

Tips #3: You can either click a Guess Tile or type the matching letter on your keyboard.
